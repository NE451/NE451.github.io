Might and Magic VI: The Mandate of Heaven

New World Computing - 1998 - PC

Might and Magic. I do remember as a kid reading all about this videogame in the September 98' issue of PC Gamer Italia. I was so interested in that game between the few screenshots in the review and the walkthrough at the end of the same issue. I miss that type of magazine, especially the CD bundles.

As I have a really sweet spot for RPGs I decided to invest a couple of hours in this game to see if it was any good: here I am, 85 hrs later it has become one of my favourite RPG ever.


A fire spirit in Gharik's Forge.
